Careers at PolicyVeda

Join our insurance advisory team

PolicyVeda is growing, and we are always looking for motivated individuals who believe in transparency, professionalism, and customer-first advisory.

Life at PolicyVeda

We offer a supportive work environment where learning, growth, and ethical practices are encouraged. Our team focuses on long-term customer relationships rather than aggressive sales targets.

Current Opportunities

Insurance Advisor

Remote Full Time

Role Overview:

Assist customers in understanding insurance products and guide them toward suitable policy options based on their needs.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Understand customer insurance requirements and financial situations
  • Explain insurance concepts, terms, and policies clearly
  • Provide accurate and unbiased policy guidance
  • Maintain a customer-first approach in all interactions
  • Stay updated with insurance industry developments

Preferred Skills:

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Basic knowledge of insurance products and concepts
  • Willingness to learn and grow in insurance advisory
  • Customer-focused attitude and ethical approach
  • Ability to explain complex information simply

Customer Support Executive

India Full Time

Role Overview:

Support customers with insurance queries, renewals, and general assistance to ensure smooth service delivery.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Handle customer inquiries professionally and efficiently
  • Assist with basic policy-related questions and information
  • Coordinate with advisory teams for complex customer needs
  • Maintain customer records and follow up as needed
  • Provide general support for policy renewals and updates

Preferred Skills:

  • Good communication skills in English and Hindi
  • Customer service orientation and patience
  • Basic computer and documentation skills
  • Ability to work in a team environment
  • Willingness to learn about insurance products
