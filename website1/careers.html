Insurance Advisor

Remote / Work from Anywhere Full Time Flexible Hours

Role Overview:

Assist customers in understanding insurance products and guide them toward suitable coverage options based on their specific needs and financial situation. This is a customer-facing advisory role requiring empathy, clarity, and deep insurance knowledge.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Understanding customer insurance requirements through detailed, empathetic discussions
  • Explaining insurance policies, terms, and conditions in clear, simple language
  • Providing need-based guidance and personalized recommendations
  • Comparing policy options from multiple insurance providers objectively
  • Supporting customers throughout the decision-making process
  • Maintaining detailed records of customer interactions and requirements
  • Providing post-selection support and guidance
  • Staying updated with insurance industry developments and regulations

Qualifications & Requirements:

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Basic understanding of insurance products and concepts (training provided)
  • Customer-first attitude and ethical approach to advisory
  • Willingness to learn and develop insurance expertise
  • Ability to explain complex concepts simply
  • Patience and empathy in customer interactions
  • Basic computer literacy and documentation skills

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive insurance advisory training program
  • Competitive compensation with performance incentives
  • Flexible working hours and location
  • Career growth opportunities in insurance advisory
  • Supportive team environment with regular coaching
  • Industry certification support

Customer Support Executive

Mumbai, India Full Time Shift-based (9 AM - 6 PM)

Role Overview:

Support customers with basic insurance queries, renewals, and information requests while ensuring excellent service delivery. This role serves as the first point of contact for customers seeking insurance information.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Handling customer communication across multiple channels (phone, email, chat)
  • Resolving general insurance queries and providing accurate information
  • Coordinating with advisory teams for complex customer needs
  • Managing policy renewal reminders and follow-ups
  • Maintaining customer records and interaction history
  • Escalating complex issues to appropriate teams
  • Providing basic policy information and documentation
  • Assisting with appointment scheduling for advisory sessions

Qualifications & Requirements:

  • Excellent communication skills in English and Hindi (other languages a plus)
  • Customer service orientation and patience in handling queries
  • Basic computer skills and system navigation
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks efficiently
  • Willingness to learn about insurance products
  • Positive attitude and problem-solving approach
  • Ability to work in a team environment

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive training in insurance basics and customer service
  • Stable work environment with fixed hours
  • Career progression opportunities
  • Health insurance and other benefits
  • Regular performance feedback and coaching
  • Opportunity to learn insurance advisory skills
